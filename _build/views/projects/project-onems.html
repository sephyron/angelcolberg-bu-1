Campus Signage

Redesign of Microsoft's internal brands under a unified Metro driven design.

New designs, content and branding guidelines were created to help unify Microsoft under a new identity that aimed to help break down the internal silos and increase cohesion across the company. This project was at the tail end of other foundational projects like OfficeTalk and NGI, which laid the groundwork with a centralized information model, profile store and messaging framework.
