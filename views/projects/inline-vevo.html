Onboarding for retention

In order to unify the experience across all our devices we decided to do an expansive redesign with the explicit goal of increasing retention and relevance.

The first time user experience for onboarding new users unto our recommendation engine and to prove out a hypothesis that would increase our engagement and retention by having users create playlists. This was based on quantitative behavioural observations and qualitative follow up interviews with some of those same users.
